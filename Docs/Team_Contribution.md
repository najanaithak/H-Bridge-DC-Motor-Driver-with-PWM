# Team Contribution

## Project Overview

The **H-Bridge DC Motor Driver with PWM** project was developed collaboratively by five Electronics Engineering students as part of a workshop initiative. Responsibilities were divided according to each member's area of expertise, spanning PCB design, circuit simulation, documentation, results analysis, and project coordination. This structured division of work enabled the team to complete the project efficiently while gaining practical, hands-on experience in motor control systems, hardware design, simulation tools, and technical documentation. The collective effort of all members ensured that the project met both technical and academic requirements within the given timeframe.

## Contribution Summary

The project responsibilities were distributed across PCB design, circuit simulation, documentation, GitHub management, project coordination, results preparation, and quality assurance. Each team member focused on a specific domain aligned with their strengths, which minimized overlap and ensured thorough coverage of all project requirements. This structured division of labor improved overall efficiency, encouraged consistent teamwork, and ensured the timely and successful completion of the workshop project.
